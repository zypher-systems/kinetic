# Terms of Service for ZypherOS Kinetic

**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

## 1. Nature of the Software
ZypherOS Kinetic (based on Fedora 44) is provided as a neutral, open-source operating system intended for general-purpose computation. The software is a downstream project that utilizes the stability and testing of its upstream providers while implementing custom branding and configurations.

## 2. Privacy-First & Zero-Data Architecture
* **Neutrality:** ZypherOS Kinetic is designed to be a neutral environment for software execution and does not function as a regulatory agent for third-party services.
* **Stateless Identity:** The OS is technically designed to be stateless regarding user biological data or age-related identity.
* **No Age-Verification Signal:** ZypherOS Kinetic explicitly does not implement the `org.freedesktop.AgeVerification1` D-Bus interface or any equivalent OS-level age-assurance signals.
* **Data Minimization:** We do not collect, store, or transmit age-verification data, as doing so would require the collection of sensitive private information, which is a violation of our core security and privacy principles.

## 3. Authorized Use & Jurisdictional Restrictions
* **User Responsibility:** Compliance with local laws regarding digital age verification is the sole responsibility of the end-user and the specific service providers they access.
* **Jurisdictional Limitation:** Use of ZypherOS Kinetic is not authorized in jurisdictions that mandate the implementation of OS-level age-assurance mechanisms or hardware-level identity tracking.
* **Liability Shift:** Users who install ZypherOS Kinetic in regions such as California (governed by AB-1043) or other territories requiring digital age assurance do so at their own risk and assume all legal liability for non-compliance with local platform regulations.

## 4. Upstream & Telemetry Stance
* **Upstream Alignment:** While ZypherOS Kinetic follows the security defaults of Fedora 44, we reserve the right to explicitly disable, remove, or "strip" any identity-shaping telemetry or age-tracking interfaces that may be merged into core upstream packages.
* **Modification:** Users are encouraged to modify the system using traditional DNF package management to further enhance their own privacy needs.

## 5. Disclaimer of Liability
* **"As-Is" Provision:** ZypherOS Kinetic is provided "as-is" without any warranties.
* **Indie Developer Status:** As an independent project, ZypherOS Kinetic does not meet the criteria for a "commercial platform" at scale and operates under a "reasonableness" standard regarding regulatory compliance.
* **Regulatory Penalties:** ZypherOS Kinetic and its developers are not responsible for fines or legal actions resulting from the lack of age-verification signals in the operating system.
